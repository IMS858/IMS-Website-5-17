Private Coaching

1-on-1 Coaching Built Around Your Body, Your Goals, and Your Life.

Coaching at IMS is not a program handed to you on a clipboard. Every session is designed specifically for you — based on how you move, what your goals are, and what your body is ready to handle.

Working directly with Jason Patterson, you get expert coaching in a private studio where every rep matters and nothing is random.

Coaching is solo, by design. You won't be passed to a junior coach. You won't share a coach's attention with three other clients. The work is personal, the program is yours, and the standard never drops.

Who it's for

  • Adults who want to get stronger but aren't sure where to start safely
  • People recovering from injury, surgery, or chronic pain
  • Former athletes ready to reclaim strength and performance
  • Anyone who has tried generic programs and isn't getting results
  • Busy professionals who want efficient, purposeful sessions

What's included

  • Free Movement Assessment before your first training session
  • Customized programming, updated as you progress
  • CARs-based joint preparation built into every session
  • Strength training through safe, coached movement patterns
  • Mobility and FRC-based work integrated into your plan
  • Body composition tracking at intake and every 8–12 weeks
  • Recovery coaching, plus Recovery Room access for members
